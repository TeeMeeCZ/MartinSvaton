About me

Young professional with Master's double degree program in International business and Economics
who graduated with honors and built cross-cultural experience while working at Deloitte, Kraft Heinz, EY, and SAP.
Looking forward to my next challenges. Here is a list of my favourite projects or achievements:

Optimization of supply chain

At Kraft Heinz, I took the initiative to improve my analytical thinking and ownership by creating an analysis that led to reduction of transportation costs. This involved mining, analysis and visualisation of data from different teams.

Analysis of startup infrastructure

At EY, I had opportunity to analyse and evaluate the Prague startup ecosystem. This included quantitative & qualitative research, development of key recommendations, and presenting them to the client.

ERP implementation

At Deloitte, I was part of the team to drive organizational change by implementing SAP Ariba for FMCG company. We used agile approach for solution design, customer journey, adoption, user testing, and go-live.

Extracurricular activies

My academic engagement (including serving as Chair of Academic Senate, student mentoring, organizing networking events, and spending multiple semesters abroad) have enriched my personal growth.
